The Details

  • Dates & Times

    Day 1 of the ride launches Friday, February 10th 8AM

    Day 2 will launch Saturday the 11th 8AM.

    We will be returning to Phoenix, Sunday February 12th by mid day.

  • Location

    Start: 7007 W Happy Valley Rd. Peoria, Arizona

    Night 1: Ajo, Arizona

    Finish: Puerto Peñasco (Rocky Point), Mexico

  • Lodging

    Based on registration numbers we are still determining accommodation for lodging in Ajo, AZ Friday night, check back soon for specifics. Saturday night we will be staying at the 1Mission Base Camp property in their new bunk houses. Details on the base camp can be found here. You will need to provide your own sleeping gear that will be transported for you to both locations.

  • Transportation Home

    We will be returning home to Phoenix on Sunday, February 12th. The 207 Miles team will provide transportation home for all riders If you prefer to return home Saturday night you will need to provide your own transportation. If this is the case please note this on your registration.

    $135.00 if registered after 1/6/2012

    Registration fees are non refundable and non transferable.

    Official Race Jersey, Event T-Shirt, Swag Bag, Fuel, Nutrition and Aid at the Start, along the Course, and at the Finish. Price also includes meals during the ride.
